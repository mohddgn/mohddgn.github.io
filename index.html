Muhammed Dogan

Environmental Scientist

I am an Environmental Scientist based in Abu Dhabi, working across environmental consulting, ecological fieldwork, and environmental data analysis.
My work focuses on Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA), Environmental Baseline Studies (EBS), and biodiversity monitoring across marine, coastal, and terrestrial ecosystems in the UAE and Qatar.
I have contributed to multidisciplinary environmental projects involving habitat assessments, ecological surveys, and environmental monitoring.
My work supports regulatory submissions aligned with EAD, DM, DECCA, and ISO 14001 frameworks, translating complex environmental data into structured, regulatory-ready insights.
My work sits at the intersection of field ecology and environmental data analysis.
I apply statistical approaches to biodiversity datasets, working with large-scale environmental data to identify patterns, quantify ecosystem dynamics, and support evidence-based environmental assessment.
