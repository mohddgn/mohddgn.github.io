Starting My Journey in Environmental Data Analysis

Recently, I began exploring environmental data analysis using R, focusing on biodiversity and ecological datasets. Coming from a primarily environmental science background, transitioning into data-driven approaches has been both challenging and rewarding.

This journey is not only about learning tools, but also about understanding how data can support better environmental decisions and reveal patterns that are not immediately visible in the field.

Fieldwork vs Data: Two Sides of Environmental Science

Fieldwork provides the raw observations — the real-world context of ecosystems — while data analysis allows us to interpret and scale those observations. Both are essential, and the most effective environmental work happens when these two approaches are combined.
