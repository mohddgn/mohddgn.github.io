About

Background

I am an Environmental Scientist with a background in marine and environmental sciences, with academic and professional experience across the UAE and Qatar. My work spans environmental consulting, research, and field-based ecological assessments.

I have contributed to environmental impact assessments (EIA), environmental baseline surveys (EBS), and marine monitoring projects, working across coastal, terrestrial, and urban environments. My experience includes both independent and team-based roles in consulting, government, and research settings.

Approach

My approach combines field observation, scientific analysis, and structured reporting. I focus on understanding environmental systems through both practical fieldwork and analytical methods, aiming to translate complex ecological data into clear, actionable insights.

Experience Highlights

  • Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA) and Environmental Baseline Surveys (EBS)
  • Marine and coastal ecosystem monitoring
  • Biodiversity surveys and habitat assessments
  • Plankton and marine community analysis
  • Environmental reporting and regulatory documentation
