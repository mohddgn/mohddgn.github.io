About

I am an environmental scientist with experience across research, environmental consulting, and field-based ecological work in the UAE and Qatar. My work focuses on understanding environmental systems through a combination of field observation, laboratory analysis, and data-driven approaches.

I have contributed to environmental impact assessments, biodiversity surveys, and marine monitoring projects, working across coastal, terrestrial, and urban environments. My experience spans consulting, academic research, and governmental contexts, allowing me to approach environmental challenges from both practical and analytical perspectives.
